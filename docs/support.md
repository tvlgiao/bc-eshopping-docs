# Support

If you encounter any issues, email us at **<contact@papathemes.com>**.

Our working hours are Monday to Friday, **8 AM to 5 PM (GMT+8)**.

For faster responses, include:

- Your store URL (`*.mybigcommerce.com` is fine)
- A short description of what you're trying to do
- A screenshot of the problem
- The theme version (Storefront → My Themes → click the theme card)

## We support

- **Comprehensive documentation** so you can install and configure eShopping yourself to mirror any of our 4 demo stores.
- **Questions** about theme usage, settings, and configurations.
- **Translation guidance** for the language files in `lang/`.
- **Bug fixes** in our original theme. (If the theme has been edited, we can only fix issues in the un-edited original — see [Update guide → Updating a customized theme](update.md#updating-a-customized-theme).)

## We do not support

- **BigCommerce platform features** unrelated to our theme (Settings, Checkout, Apps, third-party integrations, payment gateways, etc.). Contact [BigCommerce Support](https://support.bigcommerce.com/) for those.
- **Updating themes that have been customized or modified**. Migrating custom code to a new version of the theme is a paid service.
- **Editing the theme's source code on request**. If you need additional features, [contact us for a custom quote](#custom-services).

## Custom services

- **Theme setup service** — we'll install + configure the theme to match a demo, customize colors/copy, and hand you a finished store. Email for a quote.
- **Custom development** — bespoke features, integrations, layouts.
- **Migration from another theme** — we'll move your widgets and customizations across.

Email <contact@papathemes.com> with details, and we'll respond within 1 business day.

## Other channels

- **Email** — <contact@papathemes.com> is the primary support channel for bug reports and questions. Please include the details listed at the top of this page.
